Chemical
Analyst Performs chemical and physical analyses on raw materials, intermediates, and finished products using instrumental techniques (HPLC, GC, etc.) to verify compliance with quality specifications.
Microbiological
Analyst Conducts microbiological tests (microbial counts, pathogen detection, sterility tests, endotoxin assessments) on product samples, raw materials, water, and through environmental monitoring to ensure microbiological safety.
Line Quality
Assurance Monitors real-time compliance with GMP and procedures on the production/packaging line. Performs in-process controls (IPC), reviews batch documentation, and manages minor deviations.
Sterility
Assurance Specialist focused on ensuring the sterility of products and aseptic processes. Manages sterilization process validations, environmental monitoring in classified areas (cleanrooms), and supervises aseptic practices.
Process QA
Engineer A Quality Assurance professional who analyzes and improves production processes. Involved in process validation, deviation management, investigations, and implementing corrective/preventive actions (CAPA) to enhance process robustness and compliance.
